FAQ

Got
Questions?

How is pricing determined?
+
There are no fixed prices because every request is different. A basic theme setup is completely different from a custom full-funnel build. Once you submit your request, we review it within 24 hours and send a tailored quote based on the scope and complexity of the work involved. No surprises, no upselling.
How long does it take to get my service delivered?
+
Delivery time depends on the service. Simple setups like BM configuration or product research can be done within 2–4 days. More involved projects like full store builds or funnel setups typically take 5–10 business days. We'll always give you a clear timeline in your quote before you commit.
Can I order more than one service at the same time?
+
Absolutely. A lot of people come to us for a theme plus ad creatives, or a store audit followed by an email marketing setup. Just mention everything you need in your request and we'll scope and quote it all together. You may even get a better combined rate.
What if my service isn't listed?
+
That's what the custom request form is for. The listed services are just the most common ones — we handle everything across the dropshipping and ecom space. If it relates to your store, your ads, or your business structure, there's a very high chance we can help. Just describe it and we'll tell you.
Is this suitable for complete beginners?
+
Yes. Many people who use this are just starting out and want a specific part handled professionally — a clean store, legal business setup, or a set of creatives to test. You don't need to know everything. That's literally what this service is for.
Can I upgrade to full mentorship later?
+
Yes — and many people do. À-la-carte services are a great way to see the quality of our work before committing to the full program. If you want to upgrade, just let us know and we'll walk you through the mentorship options. Previous service payments may be applied as credit.
What happens if I'm not satisfied with the work?
+
We don't close out a project until you're happy. If something isn't right, we revise it. Our reputation is built on real results, not one-and-done transactions. We stay in communication throughout the entire delivery process so issues rarely arise — but when they do, we fix them.
How do I pay and is it secure?
+
Payment details will be discussed once we've sent you your quote and you're ready to move forward. We typically work with milestone-based payments for larger projects — you pay a portion upfront and the rest on delivery. We'll walk you through everything clearly before any money changes hands.
